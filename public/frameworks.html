Reporting Frameworks We Work With

Singapore SMEs are increasingly asked to report against one or more of these — often by SGX, banks, or larger buyers in their supply chain.

FrameworkWho It's ForTypical Trigger
SGX Sustainability Reporting SGX-listed companies Mandatory annual reporting
GRI Standards SMEs with international buyers Supply chain / customer ESG requests

TCFD-aligned Climate Disclosure Larger non-listed companies Board-level climate risk reporting
